# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal website of Adrián Corbalán
(`adriancorbalan.github.io/adriancorbalan`) and all games developed and published by Adrián Corbalán,
available on platforms including Steam, Epic Games Store, itch.io, and consoles.

Adrián Corbalán is committed to protecting your privacy.
This document explains clearly what data is — and is not — collected.

---

## 2. Data Collection

**No personal data is collected.**

The games developed by Adrián Corbalán do **not** collect, store, transmit,
or process any personal information, including but not limited to:

- Names, email addresses, or contact details
- Device identifiers or hardware information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Purchase history or payment information
- Account credentials

---

## 3. Third-Party Platforms

Games are distributed through third-party platforms (Steam, Epic Games Store,
itch.io, Nintendo eShop, and consoles). Each platform operates under its own privacy policy
and may collect data independently when you use their services.

Adrián Corbalán has no access to, and is not responsible for, data collected
by these platforms. Please review their respective privacy policies:

- [Steam Privacy Policy](https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/)
- [Epic Games Privacy Policy](https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy)
- [itch.io Privacy Policy](https://itch.io/docs/legal/privacy-policy)
- [Nintendo Privacy Policy](https://www.nintendo.com/en-gb/Legal/Privacy-Policy/Privacy-Policy-637785.html)

## 4. Website

This website (`adriancorbalan.github.io/adriancorbalan`) is hosted on GitHub Pages.
GitHub may collect standard server log data (such as IP addresses) as part
of their infrastructure. Please refer to the
[GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
for details.

No cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools are used on this website.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The games and website are not directed at children under the age of 13.
Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of unintentional
collection of children's data.
